Welcome to Pentago Game!
What is your name?Hop
To win: get five marbles in a row, in any direction.
Placing one marble: [Block]/[Position] [Block][Direction]
anywhere on the board and twisting any of the game blocks 90 degrees,
in either direction (Left/Right).
You can place your marble on one game block and twist any other game block.
For example: 1/1 1L or 1/1 2R
